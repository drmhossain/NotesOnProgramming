## The first 20 hours -- how to learn anything | Josh Kaufman | TEDxCSU
   https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5MgBikgcWnY

 Technics/Steps:
 # PUT MINIMUM 2O HRS OF FOCUSED DELIBERATE PRACTICE INTO THAT LESSON OR THING!
 imp: PRACTICE PRACTICE PRACTICE TO ACHIEVE THE CONCERN SKILL
 > 1. DECONSTRUCT THE SKILL:
    Clearly find out exactly what you want to know or achieve.
    reduce/break it down into pieces to its constituent parts.
 > 2. LEARN JUST ENOUGH TO SELF CORRECT WHILE PRACTICING
    Get good enough but not huge resources primarily about what it is you are trying to learn,
    but don't use those as a way to procrastinate (গড়িমসি করা, আজকাল করা) on practice.
    resources যাই হোক বই, কোর্স, সিডি অথবা অন্য কিছু তা সব শেষ করে তারপর practice শুরু করবো 
    তা না করে জানা চলতে থাকবে তার সাথে practice করার জন্য যেটুকু জানা দরকার তা জানা হওয়া
    মাত্রই practice অবশ্যই শুরু করে দিতে হবে।  
    Learning becomes a way of getting better and better at noticing when  
    you're making a mistake and doing something a little different
      
 > 3. REMOVE PRACTICE BARRIERS: 
      Overcoming destruction, Avoiding screen times like tv, phone, gossip, unnecessary meeting etc
      - if needed applying will power to achieve the concentration is a very good measure.
